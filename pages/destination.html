Explore Destinations

Taj Mahal, Agra

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, is an iconic symbol of love and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal, the majestic white marble mausoleum is renowned for its stunning architecture and intricate carvings.

Specialties: Magnificent marble structure, intricate inlay work, beautiful gardens.

Jaipur, Rajasthan

Jaipur, the Pink City of Rajasthan, is known for its vibrant culture, majestic forts, and palaces. The city is a captivating blend of history and modernity, offering visitors a glimpse into the royal heritage of Rajasthan.

Specialties: Hawa Mahal, City Palace, vibrant markets, traditional Rajasthani cuisine.

Goa

Goa, a popular beach destination, is known for its sandy shores, vibrant nightlife, and Portuguese-influenced architecture. It offers a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure for tourists.

Specialties: Beautiful beaches, water sports, vibrant nightlife, Portuguese heritage.

Mysuru Palace, Karnataka

Mysuru Palace, located in Karnataka, is a magnificent historical palace known for its Indo-Saracenic architecture. It is a symbol of Mysuru's royal heritage and is a popular tourist attraction.

Specialties: Architectural beauty, royal history, vibrant cultural events.

Hampi, Karnataka

Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is an ancient city known for its captivating ruins and temples. It provides a glimpse into the rich history of the Vijayanagara Empire.

Specialties: Ancient ruins, Virupaksha Temple, Vijayanagara architecture.

Kerala Backwaters

The Kerala Backwaters offer a serene and picturesque network of canals, lakes, and rivers. It is a popular destination for houseboat cruises and a unique way to experience the beauty of Kerala.

Specialties: Houseboat cruises, lush green landscapes, traditional Kerala culture.

Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

Varanasi, situated on the banks of the Ganges, is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world. It is a sacred city for Hindus and is known for its spiritual significance.

Specialties: Ghats along the Ganges, spiritual ceremonies, ancient temples.
